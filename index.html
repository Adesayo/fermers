About us

Fermers is a non-profit oriented and non-governmental organization with the aim to bring about a rise in female agricultural interest and eradicate gender ineqality in the Nigerian agricultural sector.

We are committed to improving the representation of women in fhe food and agricultural industries through trainings, empowerment schemes and mentorship programmes.
